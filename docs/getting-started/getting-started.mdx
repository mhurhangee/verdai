> **Quick Tip:** If you're in a hurry, check out our [Quick Start](#quick-start) section below.

## What is Verda?

Verda is a powerful file and project management system designed to help teams organize their digital assets efficiently. Its intuitive interface and robust features make it easy to store, categorize, and share files within your organization.

## Key Features

- **Project Management**: Organize your work into projects
- **File Management**: Upload, organize, and share files
- **Collaboration**: Work together with your team
- **Tagging System**: Easily categorize and find your content

## Quick Start

Follow these steps to start using Verda:

1. **Sign up** for an account
2. **Create your first project** by clicking the "+" button in the Projects section
3. **Upload files** to your project
4. **Invite team members** to collaborate

> **Important:** Make sure to set up proper permissions for your team members to control access to sensitive files.

## System Requirements

Verda works best with modern browsers:

```
- Chrome 90+
- Firefox 88+
- Safari 14+
- Edge 90+
```
